# Example Due Diligence Questions

## Commercial

* What is the customer concentration?
* Who are the largest customers?
* Which contracts contain change-of-control clauses?

## Financial

* How has revenue developed over the last three years?
* What is the EBITDA margin?
* Is there a financial forecast?

## Legal

* Are there any ongoing legal disputes?
* Are there material compliance risks?

## HR

* How many FTEs does the company have?
* Who is the key management? 

## Strategy

* What growth initiatives are described by management?
* Which strategic risks are highlighted in the documents?
